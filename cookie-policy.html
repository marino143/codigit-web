Cookie Policy

Last updated: 23 March 2026 · CO DIGIT d.o.o., Zagreb, Croatia

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device (computer, phone or tablet) when you visit it. They allow the site to "remember" certain information about your visit — such as your preferences — which improves your user experience.

2. Which cookies do we use?

Our website codigit.hr uses only necessary (technical) cookies. We do not use tracking, analytics or marketing cookies without your explicit consent.

Name Type Purpose Duration
cookieConsent Functional Stores your decision to accept or decline cookies so the banner is not shown on every visit. Permanent (localStorage)

Note: cookieConsent is not a classic cookie but a value stored in your browser's localStorage — it is not sent to any server and does not track you across domains.

3. Third-party services

The contact form uses Web3Forms (web3forms.com) to deliver messages. Web3Forms receives the data you enter in the form (name, email, message) and forwards it to our email. Web3Forms does not set cookies on your device.

The website loads fonts via Google Fonts. We recommend reviewing the Google Privacy Policy for more information.

4. Managing cookies

You can manage cookies and localStorage data through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

By clearing local data for codigit.hr you will also remove the stored consent, and the cookie banner will appear again on your next visit.
